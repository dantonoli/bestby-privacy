BestByMe – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

BestByMe is a food expiry and shopping tracker. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Local Data

All pantry items, shopping list entries, and master list items are stored locally on your device in a SQLite database. No account is required. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data.

AI Scanning (Receipt, Voice, Fridge)

When you use receipt scan, voice scan, or fridge scan, BestByMe sends the following to our AI processing server (pantry-vision-worker.dantonoli.workers.dev, hosted on Cloudflare):

  • A compressed image (receipt or fridge photo), or
  • A short audio recording (voice scan)

A random device identifier is included with each request solely to enforce free-tier usage quotas. No name, email, or personally identifiable information is attached. Images and audio are processed immediately and not stored on our servers.

Barcode Scanning

When you scan a barcode, BestByMe queries the Open Food Facts public API with only the barcode number to retrieve product information. No personal data is included. See their privacy policy for details.

In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed via RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives purchase transaction data necessary to validate and manage your subscription. See their privacy policy for details.

Notifications

Expiry notifications are generated entirely on your device and never transmitted anywhere.

Camera & Microphone

Camera access is used for barcode scanning, receipt scanning, and fridge scanning. Microphone access is used for voice scanning. No media is stored or shared beyond the AI processing request described above.

Analytics & Advertising

None. BestByMe contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no tracking.

Children

BestByMe does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes

🗓️ BestBy – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

BestBy is a food expiry tracker. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data BestBy handles and how.

Data We Collect

None. BestBy stores all your pantry data locally on your device only. No account is required. No data is sent to our servers because we have no servers.

Barcode Scanning

When you scan a barcode, BestBy queries the Open Food Facts public API to look up product information (name, brand, category). The barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts as part of this request. No personal information is included. Open Food Facts is an independent non-profit; see their privacy policy for details.

Local Notifications

BestBy may send you local notifications when items are about to expire. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

Camera

Camera access is used solely for barcode scanning within the app. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The only external service BestBy contacts is the Open Food Facts API (barcode lookups). No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included.

Data Retention

All pantry data lives in a local SQLite database on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data. We have no copies.

Children

BestBy does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If we update this policy, the new version will appear at this URL with an updated date.
